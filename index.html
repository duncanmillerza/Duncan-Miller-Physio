About the practice

Duncan Miller Physiotherapy opened in 2022. With a special interest in neurological physiotherapy, we treat people of any functional level. From stroke physiotherapy to the rehabilitation of brain injuries, our approach is a goal-oriented, problem-solving holistic approach. Based on the Bobath concept and Neurodevelopmental treatment, we focus on functional goals and look at the whole body when treating and problem solving.

Our wheelchair seating service is for anyone who is looking for a new wheelchair or needs assistance sitting better in their current chair. We work with the major wheelchair companies in Joburg to ensure that we find the best seating device for your needs.

What is Wheelchair Seating?

Wheelchair seating involves assessing and adapting a wheelchair to a personâ€™s individual needs and functional abilities. The goal is to achieve optimal posture, pressure care, and functional mobility.

What does the process involve?

  • Assessment of body, posture, pressure and functional needs by a qualified physiotherapist
  • Wheelchair seating simulation of the required adaptations or seating options
  • Prescription of posture devices or custom wheelchairs
  • Demonstration of appropriate equipment to support informed decision-making
  • Funding advice and clinical motivation to medical aids or funders
  • Fitting and adjustment of seating system
  • Follow-up assessments and review of pressure management
